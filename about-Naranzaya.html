This is me

Nickname: Narka

Age: 22

Naranzaya Batsaikhan, BSc Candidate in Software Development at the National University of Mongolia is a youth climate advocate. Trained as a youth negotiator by the Youth Negotiator Academy, she represented Mongolian youth at COP28. She coordinates youth programs at the UN Youth Advisory Panel, co-founded the Junior Climate Leaders initiative, and leads social media campaigns at Bolgoy NGO. A digital communication intern at UNICEF Mongolia, Naranzaya is committed to women's empowerment and sustainability. She speaks English and Mongolian.

My mission is to contribute to equality by addressing injustices, whether they arise from climate change, gender inequality, or other societal challenges. I believe that every step toward justice, no matter how small, contributes to a more equitable and sustainable world.
